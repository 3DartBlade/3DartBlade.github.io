Aeldari

Story

The Aeldari or Eldar have been around for much longer than humanity and they had much of the galaxy conquered before humans. All of them are potent psykers and they use their powers to power their technology. The Old Ones have developed the webway which allows for safe travels through the Warp, but it exists as a seperate dimension to the Warp. it is now used by the Aeldari both for traveling, and to live inside.
Around the 29-30st millenium the Aeldari only found entertainment in drugs, sex and torture. These thoughts, feelings and emotions however were so many at so high intensity, that they manifested a new chaos god called Slaanesh. It is the god of pleasure, pain, excess and feelings in general. After it was created, it devoured most of the souls of the Aeldari in the webway with the exceptions now capturing all types of creatures to torture in the most brutal ways, and pursuing pleasure, pain, emotions even more than before to keep Slaanesh satisfied. The Aeldari who left the webway because they didn't like what it turned into before Slaanesh's birth are now called the Craftworld Aeldari.
